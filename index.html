COVID Information Hub

Our goal in this project is to determine clinical factors of a poor prognosis in patients with COVID-19 infection. We will examine medical records of patients with COVID-19 infections confirmed by polymerase chain reaction (PCR). Logistic multivariate regression models adjusted for age and sex will be constructed to analyze independent predictive factors associated with death, ICU admission and hospitalization of these patients.
